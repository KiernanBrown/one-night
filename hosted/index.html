Slash Slay Score

Slash Slay Score is a four player game where you face waves of enemies and try to get the most kills to win. Join a game by entering a username and pressing the play button on the canvas. The game will automatically start once 4 players have joined your room. Fight through 5 waves of enemies and try to get the most kills in the game to win!

Controls

  • Use WASD to move around the screen.
  • Your character will rotate to face your mouse. Left click to slash in the direction of your mouse and hit enemies. Your slash is is followed by a period of immobility.
